What WaterFlow actually helps you do

Set a Goal That Fits You

WaterFlow calculates a recommended daily goal from your profile and lets you adjust it anytime.

Log Water Fast

Use custom cup sizes, one-tap add actions, and a focused home screen to keep the habit friction-free.

Stay in Rhythm

Reminder timing, sleep schedule, silent hours, and stop-on-goal controls help notifications feel useful instead of noisy.

Positioning: WaterFlow is a practical hydration tracker for Android, not a social challenge app and not a cloud dashboard.

Built around the features users notice every day

Widget and Lock-In Convenience

Premium users can track hydration from a real-time home screen widget without opening the app.

Wear OS on Your Wrist

Premium unlocks watch quick-add, progress tiles, complications, and mirrored reminders for supported Wear OS devices.

Progress You Can See

Statistics, streak-oriented achievements, and long-term trends make consistency easier to understand and maintain.

Privacy-first continuity: WaterFlow keeps core usage local and offers trusted device transfer tools instead of account-based cloud sync.
