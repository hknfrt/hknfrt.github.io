Privacy Policy

WaterTop is designed with privacy first. We help you stay hydrated without collecting any of your personal data.

What we collect

Nothing. We collect no personal data, no usage analytics, and no user information.

Where your data stays

  • All your hydration data is stored only on your device
  • We have no access to your data
  • We have no servers storing user information
  • No data is ever transmitted from your device

Permissions we request

Notification Permission

  • Used only to send local water reminders on your device
  • No remote notifications or data transmission
  • You can disable notifications anytime in your device settings

Device Storage Access

  • Only used by your device to store your hydration records locally
  • Adapts to your device capabilities (memory, sound, etc.)
  • No external access to this data

Data control

  • You can delete all your data anytime through the app's "Delete Data" option
  • Uninstalling the app automatically removes all data
  • Your data never leaves your device

Our principle

Simple: Your hydration data is yours alone. We built WaterTop to help you stay healthy without compromising your privacy. What happens on your device, stays on your device.
